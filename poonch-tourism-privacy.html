01

Information We Collect

Poonch Tourism ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Poonch Tourism mobile application ("App").

Information you provide:

  • Account Information: If you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture provided by Google OAuth.
  • Profile Data: Your display name and any optional profile picture URL you provide.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including pages visited and features used.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for diagnostic purposes.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymized crash data to help us improve app stability.

Information we do NOT collect:

  • Precise GPS location (we only use static map links)
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contacts, photos, or media from your device
  • Microphone or camera access
02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide the Service: To operate the App, display tourist information, and deliver the core browsing experience.
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences and improve your experience across sessions.
  • Authentication: To securely identify you when you sign in.
  • App Improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve features, fix bugs, and enhance performance.
  • Communications: To send important updates about the App or this Privacy Policy (not marketing spam).
We will never: Sell your personal data to third parties, use your data for targeted advertising, or share your information without your explicit consent — except as required by law.
03

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Google Firebase (our backend infrastructure) processes data on our behalf under Google's data processing terms.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by applicable law, regulation, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Poonch Tourism, our users, or the public.
04

Firebase & Google Services

Poonch Tourism uses Google Firebase as its backend infrastructure. This includes:

  • Firebase Firestore: Stores tourist place data, categories, and user profiles.
  • Firebase Authentication: Handles secure sign-in via Google OAuth.
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized usage statistics to improve the App.

Google Firebase's data processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase Data Processing Terms. Your data may be processed on Google's global infrastructure.

The App also integrates with Google Maps (via external links). When you tap a map link, you are directed to Google Maps and Google's own privacy policy applies.

05

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase's servers, which are protected by industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention: We retain your account data as long as you use the App. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
06

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
  • Opt-out of Analytics: You can disable analytics collection in your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

07

Children's Privacy

Poonch Tourism is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to delete such information from our records.
